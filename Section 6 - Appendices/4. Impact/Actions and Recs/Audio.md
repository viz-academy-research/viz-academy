'Previsualisation' of audio playback in a space would require a combination of all the following technologies into one solution

- Comvolution modelling of the space - ideally populated
- 1:1 Speaker Source Mapping and Calibration of Virtual and Real Spaces
- Ambisonic representation of the 'acoustic digital twin' for a headphone user
- Accurate calibration of headphones
- Head Tracking integration or headphones or additional device
- Ability to place 'ears' in different parts of the room
- Accurate calibration and modelling of trusted headphones
