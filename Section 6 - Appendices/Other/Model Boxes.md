
## Advantages

- Exist in reality - this is a familiar setting, real light occlusion and readibility
- Collaborative
- Easy superficial modification
- Established symbolic language for early stages on production
- They look cute and unintimidating

## Disadvantages

- Not a 1:1 stereoscopy on sightlines
- Hard to manufacture for 
- Hard to assess movement
- Easily breakable, hard to transport
- Difficult to version-control, substantive change is not instant

## Possible use in tandem with digital

- Micro-projection mapping
- Use of 3d-printed scale complex assets
